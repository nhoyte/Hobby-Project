Nail Design 💅🏾

One hobby I enjoy!

I got into nail design during the Covid-19 pandemic when all of the shops were closed down. I bought several nail kits and lots of supplies such as a nail drill, UV nail dryer, and plenty of polishes. I also purchased a bunch of nail art including glitters, stickers, nail wraps, and rhinestones and experimented with various new designs. I now do my own nails rather than going to shops. I love being creative and achieving the perfect design 😃!
